Convex Hull

Below is an interactive implementation of Graham Scan algorithm. Psuedo code for the algorithm is as follows: 

        Choose starting point s as the left-most point in the set. If there are multiple, choose the top most.
        Sort the remaining points by their angle from the vertical with , resulting in $p_1$,...,$p_n-1$.
        Put s and p_1 into a list L_hull
        for i from 2 to n-1
            add p_i to L_hull
            while L_hull has more than two points and the final three do not form a right turn (clockwise)
                remove the second to last point from L_hull.
